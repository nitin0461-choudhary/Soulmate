Our Privacy Commitment: SoulNotes is built on the principle that your personal thoughts and reflections should remain private. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted and never stored in plain text)
  • Account creation date

1.2 Journal Content

Your journal entries, notes, and personal reflections are stored securely and encrypted. We do not read, analyze, or process your personal content for any purpose other than providing the service to you.

1.3 Usage Information

We may collect non-personal information about how you use SoulNotes:

  • Login frequency and duration
  • Feature usage statistics
  • Device and browser information
  • IP address (for security purposes)

2. How We Use Your Information

Information Type Purpose Legal Basis
Account Information Account creation, authentication, communication Contract performance
Journal Content Service provision, backup, synchronization Contract performance
Usage Analytics Service improvement, security monitoring Legitimate interest

3. Data Security

Security Measures:
  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Strict employee access controls
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and updates
  • Secure Infrastructure: Industry-standard hosting security

3.1 Data Protection

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

3.2 Data Breach Response

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will notify affected users within 72 hours and provide detailed information about the incident and steps taken to resolve it.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We DO NOT Share Your Data

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information or journal content to third parties. Your thoughts and reflections remain private.

4.2 Limited Exceptions

We may disclose information only in these specific circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights or safety
  • In case of company merger or acquisition (with notice)

5. Your Privacy Rights

5.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectify: Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export: Download your journal entries
  • Restrict: Limit how we process your data

5.2 Data Portability

You can export your journal entries in standard formats at any time through your account settings.

6. Data Retention

6.1 Active Accounts

We retain your information as long as your account is active and as needed to provide services.

6.2 Account Deletion

When you delete your account:

  • All journal content is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Personal information is removed from our systems
  • Some metadata may be retained for legal compliance

7. Cookies and Tracking

7.1 Essential Cookies

We use necessary cookies for:

  • User authentication
  • Security features
  • Basic functionality

7.2 No Tracking

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or analytics that identify individual users.

8. Third-Party Services

SoulNotes may use third-party services for:

  • Cloud hosting (with encryption)
  • Email delivery
  • Security monitoring

These providers are contractually required to protect your data and cannot use it for their own purposes.

9. International Data Transfers

If you are located outside of [Your Country], your information may be transferred to and stored in [Your Country]. We ensure adequate protection through appropriate safeguards.

10. Children's Privacy

SoulNotes is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information immediately.

11. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify users of significant changes via email or through the service.
